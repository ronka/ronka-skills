# Mock Good Resume Example

Use this as a calibration example for what "strong" usually looks like. Do not copy its content blindly into user resumes.

## Why It Works

- Opens with a specific, non-generic summary
- Uses a standard title
- Matches likely hiring keywords
- Shows achievements, not just duties
- Includes concrete metrics and scope

## Patterns To Reuse

- Clear title directly under the name
- Summary tailored to the target function
- Bullets that combine action, context, and result
- Metrics that make the value legible quickly
- Role naming that matches real frontend/backend/software engineering job titles
