# Mock Bad Resume Example

Use this as a calibration example for what weak positioning usually looks like.

## Why It Fails

- Generic summary
- Unclear target role
- Responsibility-heavy bullets
- No metrics
- Weak verbs
- Unclear titles

## Mock Excerpt

```text
Israeli Levi

Frontend / Backend / Software Developer

Profile
Hardworking and motivated developer with experience in different environments. Fast learner, team player, and passionate about technology and success.

Experience

Frontend Developer
Media Technology Company
2025-Present
- Responsible for developing frontend features.
- Worked with backend developers and designers.
- Helped improve the product and user experience.

Backend Developer
Enterprise Technology Company
2023-2025
- Developed backend services and APIs.
- Supported different teams with technical tasks.
- Took part in solving production issues.

Software Developer
Technical Training Organization
2022-2023
- Built training materials and practical exercises.
- Worked on different software-related projects.
- Helped students understand technical concepts.
```

## Problems To Call Out

- The summary says almost nothing specific
- The title tries to cover too many directions instead of committing to one hiring target
- The bullets describe activity, not contribution
- There is no evidence of scale, ownership, or outcomes
- The wording wastes space that should be spent on proof and technical depth
